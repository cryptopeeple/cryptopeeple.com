This is The Crypto Presidents, the first digital NFT
collection designed by CryptoPeeple.

The Crypto Presidents is a unique collection of 47 tokens. Forty-six of the presidential tokens represent the 1:1 tokenization of the 46 Presidents of the United States. There is one additional token for Benjamin Franklin, the non-presidential founding father, on sale for 10,000 ETH. The other 46 tokens are not for sale, but are reserved, or set for distribution.
For this very first collection of CryptoPeeple, 40 of the 46 NFTs will be freely distributed to the owners of CryptoPunks who sign up for the website. Six of the Presidential Tokens are reserved for distribution at a later time. Both distributions will be announced at a later date in the near future.

The tile colors are represented as following:
- Green: For sale
- Blue: Current distribution
- Red: Reserved for second distribution
